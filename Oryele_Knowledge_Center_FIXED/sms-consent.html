Oryele SMS Communications

Oryele sends transactional, service-related SMS communications on behalf of professional service firms and other business customers that use the Oryele platform. Messages may relate to appointments, scheduling, client document requests, workflow notifications, engagement or case status updates, and account-related service notices.

No marketing messages are sent through this SMS program.

How users opt in

Users opt in through the Oryele Client Portal during first-time onboarding or account setup. The opt-in workflow is as follows:

  1. A user is invited by their accounting firm, law firm, insurance agency, or other business service provider using Oryele.
  2. The user accesses the Oryele Client Portal.
  3. Before entering the portal, the user is shown an SMS Communications consent screen.
  4. The SMS checkbox is unchecked by default.
  5. The user must actively check the box to consent to SMS communications.
  6. The user may continue to the portal without opting in. SMS consent is optional.

Example of the Oryele Client Portal SMS consent screen presented during onboarding. The checkbox is unchecked by default and requires active user selection.

Consent language shown to users

The Oryele Client Portal displays consent language substantially similar to the following:

SMS Communications

By checking this box, I expressly consent to receive SMS messages from Oryele on behalf of my service provider regarding appointments, scheduling, document requests, workflow notifications, account updates, and other service-related communications. Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP to opt out. Reply HELP for help. Consent is not required to purchase goods or services.

Types of messages

  • Appointment reminders
  • Scheduling confirmations, changes, and cancellations
  • Client document requests
  • Workflow notifications and reminders
  • Engagement, case, account, or service-related status updates

Message frequency

Message frequency varies depending on appointment schedules, document requests, and workflow activity.

Message and data rates

Message and data rates may apply depending on the user's mobile carrier and plan.

Opt-out instructions

Users may opt out of SMS communications at any time by replying STOP to any SMS message. After opting out, the user will receive a confirmation message and no further SMS messages will be sent unless the user opts in again.

Help instructions

Users may reply HELP to any SMS message for assistance or contact Oryele at info@oryele.com.

Privacy and sharing

Oryele does not sell SMS opt-in information. SMS consent, phone numbers, and messaging data are not shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. See our Privacy Policy for more information.

Terms

Use of Oryele messaging is also governed by our Terms & Conditions.
